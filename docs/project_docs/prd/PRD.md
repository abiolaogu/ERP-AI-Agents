# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the product requirements for a comprehensive, enterprise-grade multi-agent AI platform. This platform will serve as a complete business operations solution, enabling our company to launch as an AI agency while integrating with our existing cloud offerings. The platform will feature over 100 specialized agents across various business domains, an adaptive agent creation system, and a robust, scalable architecture.

## 2. Vision

Our vision is to create a self-adapting, intelligent automation platform that empowers businesses to streamline operations, eliminate inefficiencies, and unlock new growth opportunities. By leveraging a vast ecosystem of specialized AI agents, our platform will become the central nervous system for modern enterprises, automating everything from marketing and sales to IT operations and finance. We aim to be the leading provider of AI-powered business solutions, differentiating ourselves through a dynamic, self-configuring system that delivers unparalleled value and ease of use.

## 3. Target Customer Personas

### 3.1. SMB Owner ("Sarah")
- **Description:** Sarah owns a small to medium-sized business with 10-50 employees. She wears many hats and is constantly looking for ways to improve efficiency and reduce costs.
- **Needs:** Affordable, easy-to-implement automation solutions that don't require a dedicated IT team. She needs to automate marketing, sales, and customer service tasks to compete with larger companies.
- **Pain Points:** Limited budget, lack of technical expertise, and not enough time to manage complex software.

### 3.2. Mid-Market Manager ("Mark")
- **Description:** Mark is a department head (e.g., Marketing, Sales, IT) at a mid-market company with 50-500 employees. He is focused on optimizing his team's performance and achieving specific business goals.
- **Needs:** Powerful, customizable automation tools that can integrate with their existing software stack. He needs to automate complex workflows and gain data-driven insights to make better decisions.
- **Pain Points:** Inefficient processes, data silos between different tools, and a need to demonstrate ROI for new technology investments.

### 3.3. Enterprise Executive ("Emily")
- **Description:** Emily is a C-level executive at a large enterprise with over 500 employees. She is responsible for driving digital transformation and ensuring the company's long-term competitive advantage.
- **Needs:** A scalable, secure, and compliant AI platform that can be deployed across the entire organization. She needs a solution that can automate a wide range of business processes, from finance and HR to IT and operations.
- **Pain Points:** Legacy systems, complex regulatory requirements, and the challenge of managing a large, distributed workforce.

## 4. Feature Specifications

### 4.1. Core Platform Features
- **Multi-Agent Orchestration System:** A robust system for managing the lifecycle of agents, including their creation, deployment, and communication.
- **Adaptive Agent Creation System:** An intelligent system that can automatically discover business needs, generate custom agents, and optimize their performance over time.
- **No-Code/Low-Code Interface:** A visual workflow designer that allows users to create and customize agents without writing any code.
- **Integration Hub:** A comprehensive library of pre-built integrations with popular business tools and platforms.
- **Real-time Monitoring and Analytics:** A dashboard that provides real-time insights into agent performance, task automation rates, and cost savings.

### 4.2. Agent Categories
- **Marketing Agents:** A suite of agents for automating marketing tasks, such as SEO content optimization, social media management, and email campaign automation.
- **Sales Agents:** A suite of agents for automating sales tasks, such as lead generation, CRM data entry, and proposal generation.
- **Customer Service Agents:** A suite of agents for automating customer service tasks, such as 24/7 support chatbots, ticket routing, and knowledge base management.
- **IT Operations Agents:** A suite of agents for automating IT operations, such as infrastructure monitoring, incident detection, and log analysis.
- **Finance & Operations Agents:** A suite of agents for automating finance and operations tasks, such as invoice processing, expense tracking, and financial reporting.
- **Human Resources Agents:** A suite of agents for automating HR tasks, such as recruitment screening, interview scheduling, and employee onboarding.
- **Product & Development Agents:** A suite of agents for automating product and development tasks, such as user story generation, bug triage, and code review assistance.

## 5. User Stories

### 5.1. As a SMB Owner (Sarah), I want to...
- ...easily set up an email marketing campaign so that I can nurture my leads without spending hours writing and sending emails.
- ...have a chatbot on my website to answer common customer questions so that I can provide 24/7 support and free up my team's time.
- ...automatically post to my social media accounts so that I can maintain a consistent online presence and engage with my audience.

### 5.2. As a Mid-Market Manager (Mark), I want to...
- ...integrate the platform with our Salesforce CRM so that our sales team can automate data entry and focus on selling.
- ...create a custom workflow that automatically routes support tickets to the right team member based on their skills and availability.
- ...get a weekly report on my team's performance so that I can identify areas for improvement and track our progress towards our goals.

### 5.3. As an Enterprise Executive (Emily), I want to...
- ...deploy the platform across our entire organization so that we can standardize our business processes and improve efficiency.
- ...ensure that the platform is compliant with our industry's security and data privacy regulations.
- ...get a comprehensive overview of the platform's impact on our bottom line so that I can justify the investment to our board of directors.

## 6. Detailed Agent Specifications

This section provides a template for the detailed specifications for each agent. For the MVP, we will focus on building out one key agent from each category.

### 6.1. Marketing: SEO Content Optimization Agent
- **Goal:** To help businesses improve their search engine rankings by optimizing their website content.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Analyze existing website content for SEO best practices.
    - Identify target keywords and topics based on competitor analysis and industry trends.
    - Provide recommendations for improving on-page SEO, including title tags, meta descriptions, and header tags.
    - Generate SEO-optimized content, such as blog posts and landing pages.
- **Integrations:** Google Analytics, Google Search Console, SEMrush, Ahrefs, WordPress, HubSpot.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Improvement in keyword rankings.
    - Increase in organic traffic.
    - Higher engagement rates on optimized content.

### 6.2. Sales: Lead Scoring and Prioritization Agent
- **Goal:** To help sales teams identify and prioritize the most promising leads.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Analyze lead data from various sources, such as website forms, CRM systems, and marketing automation platforms.
    - Score leads based on a variety of factors, including their demographics, firmographics, and online behavior.
    - Prioritize leads and assign them to the appropriate sales representative.
- **Integrations:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Pipedrive, Clearbit, LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Increase in lead conversion rates.
    - Reduction in sales cycle length.
    - Higher win rates.

### 6.3. Customer Service: 24/7 Support Chatbot Agent
- **Goal:** To provide 24/7 customer support and resolve common customer issues without human intervention.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Answer frequently asked questions based on a knowledge base.
    - Create and manage support tickets.
    - Escalate complex issues to human agents.
    - Collect customer feedback and sentiment analysis.
- **Integrations:** Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, Salesforce Service Cloud, knowledge base systems.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Reduction in customer support tickets.
    - Faster response times.
    - Higher customer satisfaction scores.

### 6.4. IT Operations: Infrastructure Monitoring Agent
- **Goal:** To help IT teams proactively monitor their infrastructure and identify potential issues before they impact users.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Monitor the health and performance of servers, databases, and other infrastructure components.
    - Detect anomalies and alert the IT team to potential issues.
    - Provide root cause analysis for incidents.
    - Generate performance reports and dashboards.
- **Integrations:** Google Cloud Monitoring, Datadog, New Relic, PagerDuty, Slack.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Reduction in system downtime.
    - Faster incident response times.
    - Improved infrastructure performance.

### 6.5. Finance & Operations: Invoice Processing Agent
- **Goal:** To automate the process of invoice processing, from receipt to payment.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Extract data from invoices using OCR and NLP.
    - Validate invoice data and match it with purchase orders.
    - Route invoices for approval and payment.
    - Integrate with accounting software to create and update records.
- **Integrations:** QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, SAP, Oracle, Stripe.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Reduction in invoice processing time.
    - Fewer errors and discrepancies.
    - Improved cash flow management.

### 6.6. Human Resources: Recruitment Screening Agent
- **Goal:** To help HR teams screen and shortlist candidates for open positions.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Parse resumes and extract key information, such as skills, experience, and education.
    - Screen candidates based on predefined criteria.
    - Schedule interviews with qualified candidates.
- **Integrations:** Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, BambooHR, LinkedIn Talent Hub.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Reduction in time-to-hire.
    - Improved quality of hire.
    - Increased recruiter productivity.

### 6.7. Product & Development: Bug Triage Agent
- **Goal:** To help development teams manage and prioritize bug reports.
- **Core Functions:**
    - Automatically categorize and prioritize bug reports based on their severity and impact.
    - Assign bug reports to the appropriate team member.
    - Identify duplicate bug reports.
- **Integrations:** Jira, Asana, Trello, GitHub, GitLab.
- **Success Metrics:**
    - Faster bug resolution times.
    - Improved product quality.
    - Increased developer productivity.
