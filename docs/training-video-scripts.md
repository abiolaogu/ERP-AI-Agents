## Video 2: Creating a Workflow (3 Minutes)
**Visual**: Screen recording of the 'New Workflow' page.
**Audio**:
"Ready to automate a task? Let's create a workflow.
Click 'New Workflow'. Let's call this 'Social Media Blast'.
[Type name]
Now, let's add a task. We'll select the 'Marketing Social Media Agent'.
[Select Agent]
For the task details, we'll provide the content we want to post.
[Type JSON]
Click 'Create'.
[Click Button]
Success! Your workflow is now running. The agent is processing your request as we speak."

## Video 3: Monitoring & Analytics (2 Minutes)
**Visual**: Screen recording of the Workflow status and Analytics page.
**Audio**:
"Once you've launched a workflow, you'll want to track it.
Click on the workflow ID. You can see the status of each task. Green means complete, red means failed.
Now let's look at the big picture in Analytics.
[Navigate to Analytics]
Here you can see a stream of all events. This helps you understand how your agents are performing and identify any bottlenecks.
